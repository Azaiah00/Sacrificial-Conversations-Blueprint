GUEST PREP SHEET: SACRIFICIAL CONVERSATIONS
For: Dr. Lametra Scott
Episode: Monday · WFTB 104.1 TabNashville · Sacrificial Conversations

---

I. WHAT THIS PODCAST IS ABOUT

Sacrificial Conversations explores faith, marriage, growth, and purpose in pain. Hosts Teddy & Monica focus on real stories, sacrifice, and how God turns trials into platforms. The format is conversational—typically 60–75 minutes—broadcast live on WFTB 104.1 FM and streamed on YouTube. We want authentic, heartfelt conversation, not rehearsed answers.

---

II. EPISODE THEME

Faith Over Fear, Medical Advocacy, Motherhood, & Purpose in the Pain

---

III. TOPICS WE'LL COVER (so you can prepare)

• Your journey from misdiagnosis to founding Breaking The SSickle Cell Cycle Foundation
• Faith during pregnancy—the 25% statistic, Jeremiah 1:5, and trusting God over the odds
• The TN Health Department call and your visceral reaction ("wind knocked out of me")
• Daily life with Rickey: dactylitis, blood transfusions at age 3, school accommodations, swimming (wetsuit/heated pools), martial arts vs soccer
• Educating providers and the community—guest lecturing at pharmacy schools, churches, SCT testing
• Your "why" and the bigger picture: purpose in the pain

---

• "Rickey told his kindergarten teacher his blood cells are sticky and shaped like bananas. When did you realize he understood his condition enough to explain it to others?"
• "You refuse to let the disease rob Rickey of his childhood. How do you balance being fiercely protective as a mother without letting fear completely take over his life?"
• "You guest-lecture at pharmacy schools now—UT, Lipscomb. What do you tell future pharmacists that you wish your son's pediatrician had known?"
• "You literally built an organization to fix the exact gap in the system that hurt your family. What is the ultimate vision for 'Breaking the Cycle'?"
• "How long did it take for you to zoom out and see that Rickey's diagnosis wasn't a punishment, but a divine platform?"
• "Dr. Scott, looking back at 2012 to now, what is the greatest sacrifice you've had to make on this journey, and what did that sacrifice buy you?"
• "If you could look directly into the camera right now and speak to a mother who just got the phone call that her baby has a chronic illness... what is the first thing she needs to hear?"
